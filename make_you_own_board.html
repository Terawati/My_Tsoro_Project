Making your own board

The preferred board for a game of Tsoro is a wooden carved board. However, since itâ€™s a Zimbabwean game access to the board might be a challenge for someone living outside Zimbabwe. The first option is to look for a Mancala board online with the features described as some of the boards a similar. You could even play the game using any Mancala board but following the Tsoro game rules. In all this proves to be difficult and you want to play a Tsoro game follow the instructions below to make your own Tsoro board game.

Explore Options Below

Wooden Board

If you are into carpentry and would like to make your own wooden board

Get a piece of timber which is roughly 15 centimeters width and 1-meter-long and thick enough to carve 0.5cm cups.

Cut the timber in half and mark positions to carve 16 cups into each piece of wood.

Using appropriate tools carve your cups on both pieces of timber. Make the 16th cup bigger than all the other 15 cups.

Put hinges two hinges or use a string to join the 2 boards together so when you close they resemble a briefcase.

Find pebbles or stones or seeds or round sweats to use as pieces.

Thereafter enjoy your game of Tsoro with your home made board.

Paper Board

You can make your Tsoro board using manila paper. This method is the cheapest way to make your own board and play the game of Tsoro.
