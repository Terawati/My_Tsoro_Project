Why Tsoro

  • Tsoro is forgotten Zimbabwean game and the aim of this website is to create awareness about its existence to the younger generation. In Shona we say “kamoto kamberevere kanopisa matanda mberi”. Loosely translated my small effort can be the spark that brings the greatest renaissance of the importance of Tsoro game in our culture.
  • I would also want to share with anyone reading this website the richness of our culture and invite them to enjoy this beautiful game of Tsoro a mancala game.
